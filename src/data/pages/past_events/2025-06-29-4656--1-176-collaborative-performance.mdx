Megan Tatiana Convey Gómez & Yasaman Moussavi

1:30 PM -3:30 pm Performance|4 pm-5:30 pm Artist Discussion

The combined performance of Megan Tatiana Convey Gómez & Yasaman Moussavi on 2/1/2020 at the Beverly Arts Center will be performed in memory of both the victims of Hurricane Maria and those who lost their lives in the recent passenger plane shut down in Iran.
